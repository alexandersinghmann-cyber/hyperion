# Training Block: April 11–16, 2026

## Structure
- 6 consecutive days, full gym access
- 3 lower sessions (squat heavy / DL heavy + squat light / squat medium)
- 2 upper sessions (pull heavy + press / pull volume + press volume)
- 1 active recovery session (core + shoulder rehab)

## Key Programming Decisions
- Squat 3×/week at varying intensities to accelerate progress toward 100 kg
- Dedicated core session to address identified squat limiter
- Deadlift 1× heavy, supported by hinge accessories
- Pull:Push ratio 2.5:1 (shoulder constraint)
- All exercises on double progression
